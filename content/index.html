MyEnergy's Universal Green Button


Use MyEnergy's Universal Green Button to see how much you spend on your utility bills and find out ways for you to save money.

The Green Button is a White House initiative aimed at providing transparency to homeowners on their energy usage. Customers of any utility company should be able to securely download their utility usage, arming them with the power to make better decisions, save energy, and ultimately save money.
