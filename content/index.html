What is the Green Button?

The Green Button is a White House initiative aimed at providing transparency to homeowners on their energy usage. Customers of any utility company should be able to securely download their utility usage, arming them with the power to make better decisions, save energy, and ultimately save money. About MyEnergy

MyEnergy is a company that provides transparency to American homeowners on their energy usage. Its data delivery system expands data delivery coverage from approximately 25 million Americans to over 300 million. It allows users to download their Green Button Data with only one click. Now, you can track and reduce your home energy usage, compare usage to others, and in turn save both energy and money. Click here to learn more about MyEnergy.
